About Provenance

Provenance marks the origin of the annual techno-cultural festival of DRIEMS Autonomous College Of Engineering and Technology. This festival is an attempt to collaborate two distinctive yet very significant fields together. At Provenance, you would receive an opportunity to venture and explore your technical capabilities as well as earn a platform to brush up your artistic competence.
